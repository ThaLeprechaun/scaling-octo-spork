How To Play

  • Select Five (5) numbers ranging from 1 - 69.
  • Select one (1) number ranging from 1 - 26. This is your PowerBall selection.
  • Get your ticket (Max of 10 tickets/day).
  • Wait for the weekly draw to know the winning numbers for both drums.
  • In order to win, you must match the winning numbers from left to right.
  • As a subscriber, If by 6pm you haven't selected your numbers the system automatically selects numbers at random and enters you for the draw.
